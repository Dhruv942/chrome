Introduction

Welcome to Notification Hub. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

This application is designed to be a demo and operates entirely on your local device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All notification data is mocked and stored locally within your browser session.

  • Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information.
  • Usage Data: We do not track your usage of the application.
  • API Keys: This application may require an API key for services like Google Gemini. This key is stored locally in your browser's environment and is never sent to our servers. It is your responsibility to keep your API key secure.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use it for any purpose. All processing happens on your client-side device.

Use of Google User Data

Our app may request access to certain Google user data—such as email metadata, profile information, or content—strictly to enable core features of the app. This access is only granted after you give explicit permission through Google's OAuth consent screen.

  • We do not use Google user data to develop, improve, or train generalized AI or machine learning models.
  • All data access complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy.

Data Sharing, Transfer, or Disclosure

We do not share, transfer, or sell your Google user data to any third party. Your data may only be shared with Google APIs that you authorize explicitly for functionality (e.g., Gmail, Gemini), and only while your session is active.

Data Protection Mechanisms

All Google user data is processed locally in your browser. No information is stored on our servers. We follow security best practices including:

  • Local-only access to sensitive data
  • HTTPS-secured communication with external APIs
  • Strict handling of authentication tokens and API keys, which are never sent to our backend

Data Retention and Deletion

We do not retain any Google user data beyond your active session. Once the session ends or you close the browser, all data is discarded immediately. You may also clear your local storage or browser session to manually delete all data.

Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services like Google Gemini API to provide its functionality. Your interaction with these services is governed by their respective privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
