Welcome to NotifyAI

NotifyAI is a Chrome Extension designed to help you focus by organizing and intelligently prioritizing your unread emails, GitHub updates, Google Calendar events, and LinkedIn notifications — all in one smart inbox.

Why We Request Access

NotifyAI uses limited access to your Gmail, Calendar, LinkedIn, and GitHub accounts to display relevant information in your dashboard. This enables our AI to deliver personalized productivity tips and auto-sort your inbox. We do not store or sell your data.
